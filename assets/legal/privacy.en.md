# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: replace with your own date._

This page explains what personal data this service collects, why, and the rights you have over it. The operator of this instance is the data controller responsible for that processing.

## Who is responsible

The operator of this Forseti instance controls the data described here. Contact details are listed on the imprint page and in the operator's own policy.

## What we collect

- **Account data** you provide when you register or sign in: your email address, and any profile details you choose to add.
- **Authentication data** needed to keep your session secure: sign-in timestamps, the identity providers you link, and multi-factor settings.
- **Technical data** created automatically when you use the service: a hashed form of your IP address, your browser's user-agent string, and audit records of security-relevant actions (sign-in, consent, changes to your account).

## Why we collect it

- To create and secure your account and let you sign in.
- To authorise access to connected applications when you grant consent.
- To detect and investigate abuse, and to meet legal record-keeping obligations.

The legal basis for this processing (for operators subject to the GDPR) is typically the performance of a contract with you, the operator's legitimate interest in securing the service, and, where required, your consent.

## How long we keep it

Account data is retained for as long as your account exists. Security and audit records are retained for a limited period set by the operator, then deleted. The operator's own policy states the exact retention periods.

## Who we share it with

Personal data is shared only with the identity and authorisation services this instance is built on (Ory Kratos and Ory Hydra), any identity providers you explicitly link, and applications you grant access to. It is not sold.

## Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your data, to object to or restrict processing, and to withdraw consent. To exercise these rights, contact the operator using the details on the imprint page.

## Changes

This policy may change. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
